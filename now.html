  > What I'm working on right now

  Project: Missing Planet is the continuation   and expansion of an RPG created by a couple   of my good friends from high school. The   original was made using RPG Maker, but due   to the scope of this new game's mechanics,   we've migrated to the Unity Engine to take   more control over what our characters can do   in the overworld and in battle. For this   project, I've signed on as the lead   programmer and as a sound designer/musician.

  Link to the original game!

  Paravoid is a fledgling project I'm working   on with fellow game designers and artists I   met through the Georgia Tech VGDev Club. The   game is a 3D first person platformer with   horror elements and a strong narrative   focus. My role is the project is important   but relatively small, since the development   team is very large: I'm currently working   with the programming team on creating game   settings to customize aspect ratio and   resolution, and I plan to compose a song or   two for the levels.

  I'm writing an album! It's in its early   stages right now, but I'm hoping to release   the full thing in March 2022.
